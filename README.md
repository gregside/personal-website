# Greg Woodside — Personal Website

This is my personal portfolio site. It's meant to showcase who I am, what I've worked on, and what I bring to the table as a developer looking for new opportunities.

The site includes a brief intro, an about section with my skills broken out by category, a projects section for anything I want to highlight, and my full work history pulled straight from my resume.

It's built with React and TypeScript, bundled with Vite, and styled with plain CSS. Nothing fancy on the tooling side — just a clean, fast single-page app.
